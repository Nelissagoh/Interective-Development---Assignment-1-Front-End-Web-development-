ROCKET GRILS 101

火箭少女 101


Rocket Girls 101 was a Chinese idol girl group formed by tencent through its reality show Produce 101 (China) in 1st january 2018. It is a competition based show where hundred and one competitors have to undergo several round of elimination and ranking through showcasing there talent in different ways. After all thesestrict selections, 11 competitor were choosen, forming a C-POP group named Rocket Gril 101. They debut on 23 June 2018 with there first song "Rocket girls" However, they disbanded after two year on the 23 June 2020 as they were limited to only two years. CLICK HERE TO WATCH!
